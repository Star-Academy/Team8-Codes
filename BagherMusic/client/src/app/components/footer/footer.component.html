About

BagherMusic is a small easy-to-use website which allows users to search for their favorite artists and/or musics among our massive database powered by Elastic. Our goal is to keep BagherMusic as simple as possible, so anyone with any level of computer knowledge can easily use it. Keep in mind that all of the Bagher's products are free and always will be.
